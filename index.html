Projects

Spirit tycoon

Your average tycoon game where you can build up your tycoon by getting money from droppers! You can also collect spirits that spawn in around the map with a chance of those spirits being very rare. You are supossed to be able to use them in your tycoon, but that is still WIP.

Robloxian runner

A game inspired from the popular game speed run 4 that was made by Vurse. You run trough different levels that all have a different theme ,and if you reach the end you go trough a portal to go to the next level, you can use a menu to navigate trough the levels, and there are chat tags that you can get by buying the vip gamepass, this gamepass even comes with it's own level!

Difficulty chart simulator

A game that is all about increasing your stats, and sacrificing ceratain stats for better ones that give multipliers. You get money that you can save up to buy rebirths, but in order to do that you need multipliers so that you can get more money, you get multipliers by trading ur money for it. Then you get even more cash that you can use to get even more multipliers. Then eventually when you are rich enough you can reset all of your stats to get a rebirth that will allow you to get multipliers faster!

Long stud jump obby

A very simple game where you have to jump your way trough the stages to get to the end so you can get a rebirth, or you can go trough the bonus levels if you are really good. This game has a vip gamepass that currently only gives acess to the vip area, and a speed coil gamepass that makes you faster.
